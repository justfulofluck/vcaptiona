An alpha caption overlay is a video file with a transparent background (alpha channel) containing animated text captions. It can be placed directly on top of your video footage in editors like Premiere Pro, After Effects, or CapCut — without needing green screen removal, chroma keying, or complex masking.

Yes. Vcaptiona exports transparent WebM files with a built-in alpha channel that can be imported directly into Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, and any editor that supports WebM with transparency. No plugins or extensions required.
